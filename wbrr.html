Rules

  3. Spectators should provide only passive encouragment/ assistence until specifically requested by The Pen Holder
  4. Critisisms should like wise be reserved until requested by The Pen Holder

  6. The Pen Holder is allowed to quit at any time with no penalty and can also pause indeffinitely as is their prefrence
  7. No spectator shall be considered obligated to stay until an algorithm is solved
